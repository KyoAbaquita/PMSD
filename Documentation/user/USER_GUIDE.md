## 1. Account Management

## **1. Account Management** _(Sprint 1 Features)_

**Customer**

## 3. Task

**3. Tasks** _(Sprint 2 Features)_

### **Creating a Task**

1. Open a project ‚Üí Click **"+ Task"**.
2. Fill in:
   - **Task Title**
   - **Description**
   - **To Do**
   - **Assigned To**
   - **Start Time**
   - **Due Time**
   - **Priority**
   - **Cost**
     ![Add Task](image-4.png)

### **Assigning Tasks**

1. Open a task ‚Üí Click **"Assign"**.
2. Select a team member ‚Üí **Confirm**.
   ![Assigning members](image-5.png)

### **Updating Status**

Drag tasks between columns:

- **To Do** ‚Üí **In Progress** ‚Üí **Done**.
  ![Status](image-6.png)
